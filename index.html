Rising Tide

Market cap improvement protocol

What

The saying "A rising tide lifts all boats" is associated with the idea that improvements in the general economy will benefit all participants in that economy.

Rising Tide is a decentralized autonomous corporation (DAC) that exists to transfer fiat to the cryptocurrency economy. It consists of several algorithms that work together in parallel economies.

The DAC transfers wealth from mature fiat denominated markets to the most secure and liquid blockchain, in perpetuity.

In the mature fiat capital markets one algorithm is calculating the outcomes of profitable spread trades, while a blockchain monitoring counterpart is managing orders on multiple bitcoin exchanges. This all happens asynchronously with bridging nodes communicating how the algorithms coordinate capital (as pictured below).

Rising Tide will predictably contribute to the increase of the cryptocurrency's market cap to respectable heights.

Why

Allocation of capital into the blockchain economy is an efficient way to improve the financial system's infrastructure. The sector will be propelled to new heights as it's market cap improves. The legacy financial system has irreparable inefficiencies.

Misallocation of capital is exacerbated by unlimited issuance of fiat currency which has outpaced liquidity of legacy asset classes. Bitcoin's secure blockchain is the most efficient allocation of capital and is eligible for monetization, as it exists as the rails of a more accountable financial system.

Method

Rising Tide only creates buying pressure on cryptocurrency exchanges, with the intent to be filled. Sells are completed over the counter with strategic liquidity providers, as such, it never negatively effects the exchange rate on exchanges.

The DAC's Fiat Open Market Operations (FOMO) generate predictable cash flow based on the services it provides in the capital markets. As this cash flow is moved into bitcoin, it creates predictable buying pressure on cryptocurrency exchanges. This model is expected to be replicated - at the benefit of the entire blockchain economy - hence why portions of the DAC will be open source, but Rising Tide uses accounts created with strategic partners that enjoy the lowest commissions and rates that can be negotiated with brokers and Direct Market Access providers.

The cash flow will provide positive information on the DAC's balance sheets, which will be useful to enhance FOMO in the commercial paper market, providing additional capital that will amplify operations. The DAC's fiat capital is generating cash flow which is used to purchase bitcoin on the public markets, the fiat capital stays in fiat and generates cash flow in perpetuity.

Similarly, the DAC has existing relationships with cryptocurrency exchanges with accounts that pass all AML/KYC checks to be able to achieve the DAC's purpose.

As demonstrated, this DAC utilizes unlimited fiat monetization for blockchain monetization. Given the low float and liquidity of the cryptocurrency economy, the DAC's stated objectives will provide for returns that far outpace the percent of any one participant's contribution.

The DAC is funded by the exchange of bitcoin for tokens created using the open assets protocol. These tokens provide the utility of being able to vote on how the algorithms function. For instance, the algorithms can buy bitcoin at market price, or they can create large limit orders as the best bid which broadcasts buying pressure on the exchanges and their data visualizations everywhere, amongst other varieties of outcomes. This aspect of the DAC can be utilized in the interests of a subset of the market. These tokens can also dictate the evolution of FOMO as well, such as what markets to issue commercial paper in, and the size of the issuance.

The entity relies upon legal opinions issued to support past crowdsales, and it's FOMO entities and partners are strategically incorporated in applicable jurisdictions. The DAC is not a custodian of any customer funds, nor is the crowdsale an issuance of securities as the bearer tokens have no profit sharing value and only offer a voting privilege into a decentralized autonomous group of computer processes.
